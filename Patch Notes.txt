Patch Notes 0.05.00:
Major Updates
- Improved bullet collision significantly.
- Improved enemy collision significantly.
- You can walk through enemies for a bit when you get hit, to prevent getting stuck in the corner and killed.
- Bosses now spawn 2 upgrades and 3 HP
- Bullets now follow you to the next room
- Added upgrade slot machine effect

QoL
- Made King of Worms (Snake) attacks have more of a longer tell.
- Meat Harvester (Mantis) flying hands no longer have a hitbox, meaning they will go through you and not hit you repeatedly.
- Increased player i-frames.
- Player hitbox was too big on the bottom, updated it to be smaller.
- Number jiggle is now based on damage. flat +/- 0 to 3 => sqrt(upgradedDamageBase) / 5
- Swapped roman numerals for a simple [lv #]
- You can no longer get the same upgrade twice.

Balance
- Reduced shotgun spread. 25 degrees => 17 degrees.

Bugfixes
- Added 2 rooms back into the room selection pool.
- Shops cannot show instead of bosses anymore.
- Stop boss music when you exit a run during a bossfight.
- Bullet collision fixes a bug where shotgun bullets would pass through enemies.
- Automatically update build number on title screen, because I forgot to manually update for the last 4 versions.
